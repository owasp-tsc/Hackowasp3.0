About HackOwasp

HackOWASP is the annual Hackathon organized by OWASP Thapar Student Chapter, TIET, Patiala. Carrying the legacy forward, the fourth edition of HackOWASP, gives an opportunity to all the young innovative minds to develop financial products and discover new technologies. Being a 36-hour hackathon, the hack will begin on February 24 through offline as well as online mode and will conclude on February 26, 2022. Enthusiasts all over India will be participating in teams of 3-4 members to bring about innovation and solve real-life problems.

OWASP

Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) has been a steady source of highly skilled talent to the nation as well as overseas. A pioneer in engineering education, research and innovation. The team of OWASP Student Chapter, one of the gilt-edged coding society, involves ingenious mind solvers who are eager to make the world a better place to live in with their innovative techniques and discoveries. By successfully organizing many hackathons, tech-talks, workshops and coding nights, we have always strived hard to maintain the coding culture throughout the campus.

Benefits Of HackOwasp

Hackathons stimulate the creative thinking of participants and foster problem-solving and risk-taking in a competitive environment. In hackathons, one propels itself out of its safe haven and aspires to emanate as the best.

Hackathons provide a very good opportunity for participants to network with people from different backgrounds. During their presentation, you can know what techniques or software they used so you can understand what you are lacking in terms of knowledge and skillsets, in another way of speaking, you can estimate your competitiveness in the job market.

The skills you use and gain during a hackathon are productive in building your own impending business. The time limit on hackathons creates a uniquely productive atmosphere that forces participants to distill their visionary concepts down to actionable solutions.
